We were frustrated. Being a working student is a unique position, but the world often views "student" and "worker" as separate, with real financial tools and benefits always seeming unapproachable or for "later."

So, we combined our years of experience across academia and finance to build something different. We didn't want to create just another app trying to grab your attention while offering minimal value at a high cost.

Our vision? To build something aligned with our story, and hopefully, yours too.

What began as helping a few friends quickly revealed a much wider need. That's why we're building Renest: to assemble these smart financial strategies and bring these unique advantages to students everywhere.

Our first major step? Unlocking tax-benefits for working students. Because being a working student is an advantage. You have access to immediate and long-term benefits others don't, and we're here to help you seize them, right now and for your future.
