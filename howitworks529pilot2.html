How it Works

Sara, a PhD student in Aerospace Engineering at CU Boulder, used our calculator and discovered she could get a $924 annual tax refund using a 529 plan for her qualified expenses. Here's how Renest helps her get there:

Sara receives a $3,100/month stipend

She knows she spends ~$1,100/month on rent+utilities and roughly knows her other categories

By using Renest Sara sees her total monthly expenses by category

Renest then identifies her 529 qualified expenses and keeps a record for 3 years

Renest calculates her 529 qualified spending and how much Sara needs to deposit into her 529 account

Renest guides Sara on how to set up her 529 account and to deposit and then withdraw the amount corresponding to her qualified expenses.

Renest provides the tracking, records and calculations necessary to unlock her maximum 529 benefit of $924! By using Renest Sara unlocks her maximum 529 benefit every year effortlessly, efficiently and with confidence.

What Sara Did With Her $924 Savings Unlock

Bought 6 Domino's Pizza for a game night with friends (with a side of cheesy bread)

She bought a plane ticket to Barcelona and a couple nights in a hotel

She got started on her retirement savings and optimized her finances
