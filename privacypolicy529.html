4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Secure Cloud Infrastructure: All data is stored securely and encrypted within Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure, which maintains industry-leading security standards and compliance certifications.
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive information receives an additional layer of protection through encryption at rest, ensuring your data remains protected even when stored on our servers.
  • Air-Gapped Security: Critical decoding keys are maintained in an air-gapped environment, completely isolated from internet connectivity, making your most sensitive information inaccessible through external network attacks.
  • Data Transmission: All data transmissions between your device and our servers use industry-standard encryption protocols (TLS/SSL).
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication measures limit data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Content Delivery & Security: We utilize Cloudflare for enhanced security protection, DDoS mitigation, and secure content delivery to ensure reliable and protected access to our services.
  • Data Deletion Rights: You have the right to request deletion of your information at any time, and we will process such requests promptly in accordance with applicable privacy laws and regulations.
