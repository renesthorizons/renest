About Us

Renest was founded to address the challenges graduate students across the nation faced: high costs of living and the stress of managing money with a graduate student stipend.

Our Story

As a graduate student, co-founder Thomas Lloyd experienced firsthand the financial pressures faced by him and his peers. He reached out to this long-time friend, Thomas "Mo" Zheng, and after combing through solutions together the two founded Renest to unlock financial benefits for graduate students.

Our Mission

Our team understands the commitment and courage undertaken by graduate students. Renest aims to empower graduate students in their academic journeys and beyond.
