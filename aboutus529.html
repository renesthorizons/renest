About Us

Renest was founded to address the challenges graduate students across the nation faced: high costs of living and the stress of managing money with a graduate student stipend.

Our Story

As a graduate student, co-founder Thomas Lloyd experienced firsthand the financial pressures faced by him and his peers. Lucky for him his long-time friend, Mo Zheng, had not chosen to go into academia, but instead, industry, where he worked at prominent financial institutions. Mo and Thomas worked together to find the optimal way to structure a graduate student's finances, thereby relieving some of the financial pressures faced during a PhD program. Thomas began to share his findings with his PhD friends and soon he was helping fellow students navigate their financial challenges one-on-one, sharing insights and strategies from credit cards to retirement planning.

What started as informal peer support quickly grew through word of mouth. Students began asking if we could help their friends at other universities, and as we did, we saw the same challenges everywhere we went: graduate students needed better financial solutions tailored to their unique situations.

This realization led us to develop Renest, automating and scaling our proven approach to help graduate students gain financial security, confidence and save more money today by unlocking financial benefits. In collaboration with our trusted banking partners, we are continuously expanding our services based on the graduate student community's needs. We're excited to have you join us on this journey – your input helps shape the future of financial solutions for graduate students across the nation.

Our Mission

Our team understands the commitment and courage undertaken by graduate students. Renest aims to empower graduate students during their academic journey and beyond.
