Real Results: Sara's Renest Journey

Sara, a PhD student in Aerospace Engineering at CU Boulder, started using Renest because she wanted to build a better relationship with her money. By using Renest Sara got the clarity of seeing all her spending and budgeting in one easy to use dashboard. She also saved $924 through Renest's 529 integration without changing her spending. Sara continues to use Renest to save her time, money and build her financial confidence.

Here's how Renest helps her get there:

Sara's total income is $3,100/month.

She knows she spends ~$1,100/month on rent and utilities, and roughly knows how much in other categories.

By using Renest, Sara gets a complete, categorized view of her total monthly expenses.

Renest then identifies her 529 qualified expenses and keeps a record for 3 years.

Renest calculates how to maximize her 529 benefit, even if the expenses happened before she had a Renest account!

Renest explaines and guides Sara through easily setting up a 529 account she'll benefit from for life.

Renest provides Sara with the tracking, documentation and calculations necessary to unlock $924 effortlessly, efficiently and with confidence, each year.
