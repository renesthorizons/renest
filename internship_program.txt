Renest Internship Program

About the Program
The Renest Internship Program offers a unique opportunity for driven individuals to gain hands-on experience in a dynamic startup environment. 
This program is designed for self-motivated individuals who are eager to learn, experiment, and make a real impact.

What You'll Do

Market Research & Outreach
- Engage with potential customers and employers through both in-person and online channels
- Identify and analyze customer markets and segments
- Develop and test innovative distribution strategies
- Create and validate hypotheses about market opportunities

Project Development
- Work collaboratively on team projects
- Contribute to both structured tasks and self-directed initiatives
- Develop and test your own theories and ideas

Technical Implementation
- Participate in front-end and back-end development
- Work on website, app, and database development
- Contribute to design and visual components

Storytelling & Communication
- Develop skills in personal and brand storytelling
- Learn how to effectively communicate Renest's vision and mission
- Create compelling narratives for different audiences

What You'll Gain
- Practical experience in startup operations and development
- Skills transferable to future internships and corporate roles
- Foundation for entrepreneurial ventures
- Portfolio of completed projects
- Mentorship from experienced professionals
- Networking opportunities with industry professionals

We're looking for individuals who:
- Are self-motivated and proactive
- Can work independently while contributing to team goals
- Are eager to learn and experiment
- Can think critically and develop creative solutions
- Are comfortable with both structured work and self-directed projects
