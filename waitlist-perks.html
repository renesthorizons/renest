Mo's Career

Renest was born from a simple need. Co-founder Thomas Lloyd, during his PhD, felt the financial squeeze many students face. He teamed up with his long-time friend, Mo Zheng, who has a background in finance and a passion for financial literacy. Together, they figured out how to optimize a student's finances.

What started as helping a few friends quickly showed a wider need: students with income need financial solutions built for *them*. That's why we're building Renest – to automate these strategies and bring these benefits to students everywhere.

"Uh huh, what's an example?"

Budgeting. Everyone has heard of budgeting apps. Those apps charge you $9-14/month to track your expenses and link accounts—but then that's it. The rest is up to you. Renest is different. We offer the same budgeting and tracking tools, but we don't stop there. We know the intricacies of the financial landscape and the benefits available. So, for working students, we get you cashback on those tracked expenses like rent, groceries, and utilities, all without having to change your spending. This leads to an average of $500 back each year and that's just the one advantage there's also longterm tax-advantages for yourself and your family, retirement benefits and overall increase in financial literacy and confidence. All of this more than outweight which more than outweighs the $8/month we charge (even better as we are building everything is currently free, you pay us back in feedback and hopefully if we do our job then positive reviews!).
