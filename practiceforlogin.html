How It Works

We simplify and help graduate students maximize their tax benefits through 529 accounts, which give you a tax refund for your qualified education expenses - including rent and groceries!

1

You Are a Student and You Have an income

Because you are a student and have an income you qualify to use a 529 account which is an account managed by your state government.

2

How You Benefit from a 529 Account

Your state government wants to help you while you are a student. So if you open and use a 529 account your state will give you a refund on a portion of your education expenses.

3

How You Get Your Benefit

Open a 529 account and track your education expenses. Deposit and withdraw money equal to these expenses and then when you file your taxes your state will refund you. We will help you track and document everything properly.

4

How Renest Helps

Renest automates your 529 benefit. This includes tracking your education expenses, handling your deposits, withdrawals and management of your account and forms. We are confident in getting you your refund, so there is no cost until you benefit!
