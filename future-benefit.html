Frequently Asked Questions

What is Renest?

We are a startup that helps working students achieve financial confidence, starting with maximizing their benefits through 529 plans, budgeting and expense tracking.

What is a Renest 529?

A 529 plan is a special savings account for education, similar to how a 401(k) is for retirement. When you're a student you can use and benefit from a 529 just like you use your 401(k) when you retire.

Who is Renest for?

Renest is for students who have any income during the year, including PhDs, Masters's, undergraduates, community college, and trade school students. It is also for individuals planning to go back to school, for parents saving for their children's education and employees saving for family members' education.

Do I need an employer?

Renest works for students both through their employer and independent of their employer. If your employer offers Renest they are paying so that you have access to these benefits! If your employer is not offering Renest, let them know you want it and send us an email. As an individual you can still use Renest, we have a free version and a premium version to help grow your money (and skills).
