How It Works
We help students with their finances and taxes. This is what we'll ask for and do for you:
  1. 1. Based on your basic information and your state, we start to narrow down the tax benefits available to you.
  2. 2. Next, we ask for your income and education related expenses you've had this year (e.g., rent). These expenses qualify for a tax deduction in your state while you are a student.
    (The state and federal law that creates this education benefit is based on section 529, just like how retirement benefits are described in section 401).
  3. 3. We record this information so that it is ready to be submitted on your taxes. The records need to be made in the same calendar year that the expenses happen, so if you wait till next tax season (which is in the next calendar year) then you have lost the opportunity to qualify for your benefit.
  4. ✓ In April, we file your taxes and include the paperwork to mark the extra benefits you qualify for based on the information you provided and the setup we did.
  5. 👌 If you don't want us to submit your taxes, that's fine too! We'll go through the same steps and prepare all the paperwork for you to hand over to any other tax filing company or person you choose.
  6. 💰 You only pay when you file your taxes and get your maximum benefit. We gaurantee a higher benefit with Renest than you would have received without us.
