Maximizing Financial Literacy and Educational Benefits for University Employees and Students through Illinois' 529 Plan

Financial Benefits

  • Tax Credit for Employers: The University qualifies for $500 in state tax credits for every $2,000 in pay to a qualifying employee
  • Tax Credit for Individuals: Employees can qualify for up to $500 in state tax credits
  • Additional Tax Advantages: Participants benefit from various other tax advantages inherent to 529 plans

Who Benefits

  • Graduate students
  • Student employees
  • Full-time employees with children in college
  • Full-time employees saving for future children's education
  • Full-time employees planning to pursue advanced degrees (Masters or PhD)
